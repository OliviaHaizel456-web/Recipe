Cookies recipe

This is a recipe to make soft-chewy chocolate chip cookies for you and friends or family to share.

Click here to check out the full recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g Flour
  • 180g Semi Sweet Milk Chocolate
  • 1 Egg
  • 80g White Sugar and 70g Brown Sugar
  • 130g Butter
  • 30g Cocoa powder

Equipment

  • Weighing scale
  • Mixture or Spatula
  • Bowl

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat over to 180degrees
  2. Measure and add both White and Brown sugar to the bowl
  3. Melt butter in separate bowl and wait 5mins to cools down before adding to bowl
  4. Mix sugar and butter together and add egg to mixture Don't Over Mix The Egg
  5. Measure Flour and Cocoa powder before adding to bowl
  6. Mix Flour and Cocoa powder and slowly add in Semi sweet milk chocolate Note:DO NOT OVER MIX
  7. Get oven Tray out and place parchment paper in tray
  8. Grab bowl and measure the size of each cookie dough ball e.g. 20g each
  9. Put try in oven and let cookies bake for 25minutes
  10. When cookies are fully baked pull out the over and let them rest for 5minutes on try before taking them of and putting on a wire rack or use plate and then let cookies rest for another 10minutes
